More About us.

HENAMICH INTEGRATED COMPANY LIMITED is a top notch aluminium producing company that was established over 25 years ago and has been incorporated with Registration No RC: 1271359, HENAMICH is recognized by the STANDARD ORGANIZATION OF NIGERIA (SON) as suppliers of Up-To-Standard Aluminium Products.

Due to our years in service, our standard equipments/machinery, our cumulative clientele and our qualified employees, we increased our experience by providing different solutions to the problems of many engineers and clients so we have a very good reputation preceding us..

Our Mission

To become the leading supplier of high quality aluminium products at a very affordable and flexible price so that wherever any aluminium product is mentioned, HENAMICH INTEGRATED COMPANY LIMITED is mentioned.

Our Vision

Our aim is that our products maintain the build quality and pleasing aesthetics that will make us the marquee of architectural perfection in Nigeria and also to be the go-to plug for all aluminium products and services.
